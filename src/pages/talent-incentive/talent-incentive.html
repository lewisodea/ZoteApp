Talent Incentive Scheme

What is a Talent Incentive Scheme?

This is a scheme where the Company rewards it employees for recommending people in their professional networks as successful new hires into the Company.

What are the benefits of the Scheme?

  • Increased quality of hire - referrals have shown to be the most effecitve method of determining the peformance of a prospective employee.
  • Our employees positively sell our Company to others, and as a result benefit financially.
  • Its a win-win situation, the employee gets a reward and the Company benefits from alternate hiring processes.
  • Studies show that referred employees stay longger than a non-referred hire.

Your Incentive and Payment

  • The value of the incentive will be based on the basic salary and in some cases the level of difficulty to fill the position as follows:
Level Basic Salary Incentive
Level 1 Up to £30,000 £500 gross
Level 2 £30,000 and above £1000 gross

How can I take advantage of the Scheme?

  • All employees on Zotefoams payroll are eligible to participate in the scheme except the hiring manager or where the referrer has a direct impact on the hiring decision.
  • The person making the referral must be employed by Zotefoams at the time in which the incentive is due to be paid.
  • The referred candidate must not currently work or have worked for Zotefoams in the last 3 years as a permanenet employee, contractor, or on a temporary basis.
  • All referrals must be qualified leads, which means the referrer must be able to provide First Name, Surname, Phone Number, and Email Address.
  • A referral will not be eligible if a candidate has already applied for a role in Zotefoams in the last 6 months.
  • The reward will only be paid once the new employee has successfully passed their probationary period at Zotefoams.
  • Referrals are submitted via the careers page on the Zotefoams website
  • Inform the candidate that you are referring to apply online and whilst submitting their application, state your name in the 'how did you find out about this vacancy' section. If the candidate is referred by more than one person, the referral fee will be paid to the referrer who has submitted the candidate first.
  • This is a discretionary benefit and the Company may vary these terms without notice for any new participant to the scheme and may withdraw the scheme at any time.
