FAQ Page

How do I request IT support?

The best way to contact IT is via systems@zotefoams.com, this will generate a support ticket in our system.

How long can I expect to wait to recieve support?

We always endeavour to provide support as quickly as possible, however during especially busy times, the priority system will be closley followed, and your ticket may be placed on hold if it is of a lower priority.

For example, should the there be a fault in any computers that the factory machines rely on to operate, this will become the highest priority. Once high priority calls have been resolved, any available agent will aim to answer your ticket in a timely manner.

Where can I find the IT department?

If you need to visit the department for any reason, you can find us at the very end of the Factory 3A offices.

What does it mean if I am a "Primary Owner" for a Rollcall printer?

If you are a Primary Owner, your duties are as follows:

  • On the weekly fire alarm test, every Tuesday at 10:00AM, you will need to check your local printer after the alarm has finished, and check that the Rollcall Report has printed.
  • If you are not in the office on one of these days, please inform the Secondary Owner of the printer, so that they can fulfill this duty. If you are unsure of who your Secondary Owner is, please contact us and we'll fill you in.
  • Once you have checked if the Rollcall Report has printed, please email rollcall@zotefoams.com with the status.
  • You do not need to keep the report, it can be recycled once you have sent the confirmation email.
  • This procedure is carried out for Health & Safety reasons. In the event of a fire, these reports will automatically print so we know exactly who is on site, and can accurately check for any missing persons. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the procedure, please put them forward to rollcall@zotefoams.com
Where do I seek support for Microsoft AX?

For any AX queries, please contact axhelpdesk@zotefoams.com. If you are seeking training for AX, please contact the designated super-user in your department. If you are unsure of who this is, contact your manager, and they'll be able to fill you in.

I have not had an IT induction, what should I do?

When a new member of staff joins, they recieve an IT induction from us as part of the joining procedure. If for whatever reason you did not recieve an IT induction, please contact systems@zotefoams.com letting us know, and we will arrange an induction for you.
